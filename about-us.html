Tupavu Goals

Objectives :

  1. To promote the planting of trees as a lifestyle in schools.
  2. To raise awareness on the dangers of deforestation.
  3. To mobilize resources for expansion of tree cover in schools.
  4. To monitor and support school-based afforestation efforts.
  5. Offer Mentorship program in schools
