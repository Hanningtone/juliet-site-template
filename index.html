Our Goal

We are an organization with the goal of planting fruit trees and other indigenous trees in Kenyan schools therefore improving tree cover and promoting nutrition

Educate Learners on the need for green environment and plant more trees

Tupavu's

focus is to integrate innovative and transformative ideas on how to promote afforestation and improvements of tree cover in schools across the country.We work with the ministry of Education,County governments and school management in identifying schools that lag behind in matters trees. On school farms,we support learners in planting trees for;
  1. Fruits/Tunda (Nutrition)
  2. Beauty/Pambo (Beauty/Aesthetic)
  3. Tree cover/kivuli (Canopy).

The pupils/students watch and learn the proper way to plant trees and to nurture them throughout their school life. Far from providing shelter, beautification and nutrition, the fruit trees play a major role in the absorption of carbon from the environment for us all.We are determined to make major difference by 2030.

Tupavu

A Community that promotes 'All under one leaf' mantra

We encourage and teach pupils/students to watch and learn how the trees are planted;adopting the trees to care for them throughout their school life. Apart from planting trees, we also offer mentorship and career guidance in schools through the environmental club. Tupavu objectives are;

  • To promote the planting of trees as a lifestyle in schools.
  • To raise awareness on the dangers of deforestation.
  • To mobilize resources for expansion of tree cover in schools.
  • To monitor and support school-based afforestation efforts.
  • Offer Mentorship program in schools

Something about Fruit Trees!

Planting fruit trees is not only a responsible choice for our environment and food supply, it can also be a fun and rewarding hobby. Imagine picking fresh, juicy apples from your own backyard or savoring the sweet taste of a ripe peach straight from the tree. Not only will you be able to enjoy delicious and healthy fruits, but you'll also be contributing to a greener planet by promoting biodiversity and reducing carbon footprint. Fruit trees can also add aesthetic value to your garden or yard. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, from dwarf trees perfect for small spaces to towering varieties that make a statement. And let's not forget about the beauty of blooming fruit trees in spring, with their vibrant flowers and fragrant scent...
